About Us

Hey there! We're the We're Witty Wombats, a team of four students from NYC. We hope you enjoy our project, feel free to stick around!

We're Witty Wombats

What we've done here is model the impact of fast food restaurants and commercial businesses in the United States on the wealth and health of our nation.

We believe that data visualization is a powerful tool for understanding complex phenomena and making informed decisions. In this project, we have collected and analyzed extensive data on the presence of fast food restaurants and commercial establishments across the United States.

Our objective is to provide a visual representation of how these establishments affect both the wealth and health of our nation. Through interactive charts, graphs, and maps, we aim to convey meaningful insights that can inform policymakers, researchers, and the general public.

By examining various factors, we have developed a comprehensive model that highlights the correlations between the proliferation of fast food restaurants, commercial businesses, and the overall well-being of our society.

Our team has worked to collect, clean, and analyze the data. We have implemented dynamic, novel, and intriguing data visualization techniques to present the findings in a visually compelling and accessible manner.

We hope that this project encourages a deeper understanding of the complex dynamics between fast food, commercial establishments, and societal well-being. Through this data visualization, we aim to inspire meaningful discussions and actions that can contribute to a healthier and more prosperous future.

Thank you for visiting our project. We invite you to explore the visualizations and engage with the data to gain valuable insights into the impact of fast food restaurants and commercial businesses on our nation.
